About Code Forge

Our Genesis and Vision

Code Forge was officially established in 2022 as a response to the growing need for a dedicated, hands-on technology environment at **National Institute of Advanced Manufacturing Technology (NIAMT), Ranchi**. While NIAMT is renowned for its core engineering, the club was created to foster a parallel, equally robust **coding and software development culture**.

The primary goal remains to empower students from all branches to **Learn**, **Build**, and **Lead** in the rapidly evolving tech landscape. We bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, ensuring our members are industry-ready innovators.

This community is the core engine for building robust, scalable applications. We cover everything from Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA) to full-stack web and mobile development, focusing on clean code and deployment best practices.

Activities & Focus

  • Weekly Competitive Programming Sessions
  • Full-Stack Development Bootcamps (MERN/MEAN)
  • Version Control (Git) workshops
  • Open Source Contribution drives

Core Technologies

React/Next.js Node.js/Express MongoDB/PostgreSQL Python/C++ (DSA)

Projects Highlight

*College Internal ERP System, *Clubs Portfolio Website, *Job Portal Clone.
